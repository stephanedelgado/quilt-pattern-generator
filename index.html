Custom Quilt Design Facility

A practical tool to extract color palettes and determine distribution for patchwork artworks. One of quilting's most fundamental designs, this checkerboard reproduces a "Nine Patch" pattern from 1900's, likely designed in New Jersey, by an anonymous maker who repurposed wool, serge and gabardine fabrics typically destined for men's suits and coats.
