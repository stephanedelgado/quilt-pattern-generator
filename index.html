Custom Quilt Design Facility

A practical tool for extracting color palettes and determining distribution in a patchwork artwork. One of quilting's most fundamental designs, this checkerboard reproduces a "Nine Patch" quilt pattern, very popular during the Civil War for its effective use of scraps.

Feel free to reach out with ideas for making this space even better. If you'd like to support the quilting community, PayPal donations are always appreciated. Questions, feedback, and suggestions for improvement are very welcome too.
